About Me

My name is Jack Leiman. I am a rising senior at Northern Highlands Regional High School and the founder of Project Bloom. I have many hobbies, but above all else I love origami and I want to share its beauty with everyone.

What is Project Bloom?

Project Bloom stems from a story I heard where NICU rooms often seemed bare and monotone; families couldn't even bring flowers into the rooms to cheer their kids up or support them with the beauty of a bouquet due to pollen being a dangerous allergen in a place such as the NICU. At the time, I was in the process of learning to make origami flowers. I realized that if pollen was the problem, paper could be the solution. I started Project Bloom so families had something beautiful to give to their children, giving them strength and hope.
